Interactive Web Development
Reactive Roots
November 2022 - CSCI1300
The aim of this project was to create a website with a variety of interactive elements and sorting features, to gain practice using React and JavaScript. Reactive Roots allows users to add and remove items to cart, calculates total price, and can filter items by a variety of different properties.

Reactive Roots is targeted for new plant users, so every plant has a difficulty rating and users are given the opportunity to sort items by difficulty so that they have an easier experience picking a suitable plant.
